Steps

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Spread each slice of bread with butter on one side.
  3. On the unbuttered side of 4 slices of bread, spread a tablespoon of mango chutney.
  4. Layer the shredded cheese, sliced tomatoes, and sliced onions on top of the chutney.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Top each sandwich with another slice of bread, buttered side up.
  7. Grill the sandwiches over medium heat until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted, about 5 minutes per side.
  8. Slice the sandwiches in half and serve.
